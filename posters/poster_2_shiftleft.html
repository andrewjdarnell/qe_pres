Shift Left & Feedback Loops

Catch it early.
Catch it cheap.

A defect's cost explodes the later it's found. Fast, automated checks in CI turn week-long feedback into minutes — so developers learn and correct before problems grow.

The cost of a late defect

relative cost to fix

Fixing in production can cost ~100× more than catching the same defect at commit. Every stage you shift left compounds the saving.
